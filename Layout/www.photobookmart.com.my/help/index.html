Vouchers Redemption

Questions:

  2. Can I combine my orders and ship them together?

    6. Click on redemption guide below: http://www.photobookmart.com/steps.pdf

    1. I am not familiar with designing work, is it difficult for me to work with your software?

    3. What kind of picture formats can i use for my photo book?
  • RGB, CMYK or Grayscale JPEG
  • RGB, CMYK or Grayscale TIFF

    1. How do i upload my photos to the software?
      Look for the Add Images icon + at the bottom left panel. Once you click on it, a window will pop-up, prompting you to select the folder where your photos are stored. Select the folder, then your choice of photos and click ‘OK’. You can also click on the Select All button at the top left corner if you would like all the photos to be uploaded.Or if you have a full folder of pictures which you would like to upload, then click on the Add Folder with Images. All your photos are now ready to be placed in to your photo book.

      Back to top

    2. How do I put my photos on the page?
      Your photos will first appear as thumbnails at the bottom panel on Photo Book Editor. To place them in your photo book, just drag and drop them on to your desired page and position.

      Back to top

    3. What is the best resolution for photos in my photo book?
      photobookmart recommends using resolution of 240dpi and above. If your photos are of a lower resolution, they may appear blurred or pixelated in the finished product.

      Back to top

    4. How do I know if my photos are of the minimum required resolution?
      In your photo folder, right-click on the selected image, choose Properties > Details. Look for the ‘Horizontal Resolution’ and ‘Vertical Resolution’. You can then see if your photo meets the minimum requirement of 240dpi.You will then see an additional bar just above your photo book thumbnails. If you mouse over each square shown, you will be able to see what errors exist on every page – including low resolution photos.

      Back to top

    5. What if the resolution of my photos is below the minimum requirement?
      Unless you are well-versed with photo enhancing software such as Photoshop, we recommend for you to retake the photo or simply use another alternative.

      Back to top

    6. I can’t upload my photos on to the software. What should I do?
      Check if your images match the following requirements.
  • JPEG, TIFF or PNG formats. PNGs should be 8-bit and non-interlaced
  • sRGB colour (no CMYK nor Grayscale nor Adobe 1998 RGB)
  • If you’re using iPhoto, ensure that your photo library has its default name and is saved in its default location
  • 150 to 300 dpi in resolution

P/s: You can check the above details by right clicking on the selected image and then selecting Properties.
If you’re still facing problems, do get in touch with our Customer Service Department.

Back to top

    1. Does Photo Book Editor come with image-enhancing features?
      You can perform basic enhancements on your photos with Photo Book Editor such as zooming in and out on a particular portion, moving away unattractive portions or backgrounds, improving picture brightness and vibrance, adding a sepia or black and white effect on them and more.

      Back to top

    2. If I delete or alter my photos on photobook editor, will it affect the original photo?
      No. They will only appear deleted, altered or enhanced on the software, not on the original file.

      Back to top

    3. Can I change the size of my photo book while I’m halfway designing it?
      Unfortunately, you can’t. If you wish to have another size, you will need to start another new project.

      Back to top

    4. How do I add more pages to my photo book?
      Click on Insert > Append New Page. The new pages will be added from the last page onwards. You can also find this command when you right-click on the page.

      Back to top

    5. Can I insert a page in between two pages I’ve previously created?
      Not a problem! Just click on Insert > Insert New Page.

      Back to top

    6. Will the photos I’ve uploaded onto photobook editor disappear when I close the program?
      As long as you save your project before closing, no.

Back to top

  1. Do I have to remain connected to the Internet to design my photo book?
    You don’t have to be connected to the Internet unless you want to place your order or refer to the photobookmart website.

  1. How do I improve picture brightness and vibrance?
    You can either click on the Perfectly Clear Image icon at the editing taskbar or right-click on the selected photo and then click on the Perfectly Clear Image option.To remove the enhancement, do the same process.

    Back to top

  2. How do I change my photos to a sepia or black and white colour tone?
    Right-click on the selected photo, select Filter and then click on your preference. Or you can also click on the B&W Filter (insert icon) or Sepia Filter (insert icon) at the editing taskbar.

    Back to top

  3. What does the Fade command do?
    The Fade command is best used if you wish to make a photo less prominent. By selecting a higher Fade percentage, the lesser prominent your selected photo will be. Its effect is almost as if a white tinge was overlaid on the photo.

    Back to top

  4. I wish to add borders around my photos. How do I do it?
    Look for the Picture Borders option at the bottom panel where your photo thumbnails are displayed. A variety of borders will then be opened up for your selection. Select the border of your choice and then drag and drop the border thumbnail on to your photo.

    Back to top

  5. How do I remove the borders then?
    Easy! Right-click on the respective photo > Edit > Remove Border.

    Back to top

  6. Can I add a shadow behind my photos?
    Not a problem! Just make sure you’ve selected the photo to have a shadow, then click on the Add Drop Shadow icon (insert icon). You can also find this command when you right-click on the selected photo!To remove, repeat the same process – except now, look out for Remove Drop Shadow instead.

    Back to top

  7. Can I change the orientation of my photos or make them slanted?
    Sure. Here’s what you need to do:

    • Right-click on the respective photo thumbnail
    • Select ‘Rotate’ from the sub-menu that pops up. Rotate the photo to your desired position.

      Back to top

  8. How do I change the position of my photos / captions?
    It’s as easy as dragging them around to your desired position. Just be sure to select the item first.

    Back to top

  9. I don’t like one of the photos I’ve placed on to my photo book. How do I remove it?
    Simply right-click on the thumbnail and select ‘Remove Image ’or ‘Delete’ on the sub-menu that appears.

    Back to top

  10. I don’t remember which photos I’ve used or placed on to my photo book. How do I avoid accidentally repeating the same photo?
    No worries, just click on the v icon called Hide Used Images at the left side of the panel that’s holding your photo thumbnails. This will temporarily hide the images you’ve already placed on your photo book.

    Back to top

  11. How do I remove all the photos displayed on the thumbnail panel?
    At the left-hand side of this panel, look for the X sign (insert icon) called Clear Image Browser. Once you click on this, every thumbnail will be removed, including the photos you’ve placed on to your photo book. So be sure to only click on this icon when you are certain that you want to remove the photos.

    Back to top

  12. How do I include text into pages of my book?
    Click Insert > New Caption or right-click on the page and select Insert > New Caption. A text box will then appear on the page. You can double-click it to start typing or editing your text.

    Back to top

  13. How do I adjust the font type, size, colour etc?
    Double-click the respective text box and change font type, size, colour etc accordingly using the Editing task bar.

    Back to top

  14. How come I only see a portion of my text after changing the font size?
    You have probably selected a font size larger than the available text box space. You can either resize your fonts or the text box itself by dragging the small squares at the corners of the text box, in and out.

    Back to top

  15. What if I made a mistake whilst designing my photo book?
    No worries! Just press Ctrl and Z at the same time. This command will undo the mistake made. For Mac users, press Command and Z simultaneously.

    Back to top

  16. I’m not happy with the page arrangements in my book. How do I change it?
    Look for ‘Pages’, click on it and then select ‘Rearrange Pages’. You can then drag and drop the pages in the order of your liking.

    Back to top

  17. How do I add or change the page backgrounds?
    Just drag and drop till you’re satisfied with the outcome. Or if you prefer a plain background, drag and drop the background labelled as ‘None’.Once you drag and drop any background, it will always resize to fit proportionately to the page. You can change the background size by double-clicking it and dragging its corners accordingly.

    Back to top

  18. Is it possible to print the title of my album on the book’s spine?
    Because your photo book will be side-sewn by hand, printing on the book’s spine will affect the beauty and craftsmanship of this manual binding process. As such, printing your photo book’s title on the spine is not practiced by photobookmart.

    Back to top

  19. Can I save my photo book and finish designing it later?
    Of course! We wouldn’t want to rush your masterpiece. But remember to save your file first before you close the software! When you’re ready to work on your photo book again, simply re-open the program and then open your project from there.
